Ingredients:

1 egg (M of L)
1 ts almond flour
60 gr grated mozzarella
20 gr grated Gouda
butter or coconut oil

Heat the waffle iron and grease it with a little butter or coconut oil.
In the meantime, mix the mozzarella, almond flour and the egg in a bowl.
Sprinkle a little bit of grated cheese (mozzarella or Gouda) on the waffle iron and then add the batter.
Then sprinkle some grated cheese on the top of the waffle and close the waffle iron.
Bake the cheese waffle for approx. 6-8 minutes in the waffle iron.
